1. Apple Music

Premium music streaming with high-quality lossless audio.

  • 100 million+ songs
  • Offline listening
  • Spatial Audio with Dolby Atmos

Pricing: $9.99/month for individuals, $14.99 for families.

Visit Apple Music

2. YouTube Music

Stream official songs, albums, and playlists with video integration.

  • Ad-free music with Premium
  • Background play
  • Download for offline use

Pricing: $9.99/month.

Visit YouTube Music

3. Tidal

High-fidelity music streaming for audiophiles.

  • Lossless audio quality
  • Exclusive artist content
  • Hi-Res audio with a premium plan

Pricing: $9.99/month for HiFi, $19.99/month for HiFi Plus.

Visit Tidal
